Memories

Tree Conservation

On March 2, 2024, a group of students from PICT set out on a journey to Swaminarayan Hill with the primary objective of tree conservation. To fulfil this purpose, they collaborated with “Zade Lava Zade Jagva Foundation”.

Expert session on How to Live a Balanced Life

On 5th March , The session on how to live a balanced life was conducted for students. The experty for the session was Sunil Khot Sir. The session aims to explore the various facets of achieving equilibrium in our daily lives.

Blood Donation Drive

The Blood Donation Drive organized by PICT NSS was a tremendous success, showing the generous and community-minded spirit of students and staff.
